**Key Columns**:
- **date**: The date when the property was sold.
- **price**: The selling price of the property.
- **bedrooms**: The number of bedrooms in the property.
- **bathrooms**: The number of bathrooms in the property.
- **sqft_living**: The living area size in square feet.
- **sqft_lot**: The lot size in square feet.
- **floors**: The number of floors in the property.
- **waterfront**: Whether the property is a waterfront property (1) or not (0).
- **view**: The quality of the view from the property.
- **condition**: The overall condition of the property.

**Usage**: This dataset can be used for:
- Predictive modeling to forecast house prices based on various features.
- Analyzing the impact of different attributes (e.g., number of bedrooms, lot size) on the property price.
- Understanding trends and patterns in the housing market.
- Evaluating the real estate market conditions over time.
