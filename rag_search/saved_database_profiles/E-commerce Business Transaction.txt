### Dataset Name
E-commerce Business Transaction

### Overview
This dataset contains sales transaction data from a UK-based e-commerce business for one year. The data includes transactions from a London-based shop selling gifts and homewares.

### Usage
- **Sales Trend Analysis**: Identify peak sales periods and seasonal trends.
- **Product Popularity**: Determine the most frequently purchased products.
- **Customer Behavior**: Analyze the average number of products per transaction.
