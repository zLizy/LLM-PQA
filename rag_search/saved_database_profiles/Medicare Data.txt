**Dataset Name**: Medicare Data

**Overview**: This dataset provides comprehensive information on the utilization and payments for various procedures, services, and prescription drugs provided to Medicare beneficiaries. The dataset includes details about inpatient and outpatient services, physician and supplier procedures, and Part D prescriptions. It is sourced from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services and includes data such as provider identification numbers, agency names, addresses, service counts, and payment amounts. The columns in the dataset include: ['provider_id', 'agency_name', 'street_address', 'city', 'state', 'zip_code', 'total_episodes_non_lupa', 'distinct_users_non_lupa', 'total_hha_charge_amount_non_lupa', 'total_hha_medicare_payment_amount_non_lupa']. **Usage**: The Medicare Data dataset is extensively used in healthcare research and policy analysis. It is particularly useful for developing machine learning models to predict healthcare costs, utilization patterns, and health outcomes based on various demographic and service-related factors. This dataset supports a wide range of healthcare analyses, from understanding regional variations in healthcare utilization to evaluating the efficiency of different healthcare providers.

The Medicare Data dataset is essential for both academic research and practical applications in the healthcare industry. It aids healthcare professionals, policymakers, and researchers in identifying trends, optimizing resource allocation, and improving overall healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.
