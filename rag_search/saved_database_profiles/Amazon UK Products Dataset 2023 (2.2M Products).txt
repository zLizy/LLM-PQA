### Dataset Name
Amazon UK Products Dataset 2023 (2.2M Products)

### Overview
This dataset contains detailed information about 2.2 million products sold on Amazon UK, including pricing, sales, ratings, and other product attributes. The data was scraped in October 2023.

- **Product Performance Analysis**: Identify top-selling and highest-rated products.
